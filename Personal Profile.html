What do these results mean to me

The results of the above tests provide clarity on why I do some of the things I do.
Reading further into my results has given me tips on how to better study so as to not lose focus,
and shown me some things I need to be mindful of when talking and interacting with other people.
I found the Myers-Briggs test particularly interesting as it felt surreal how accurate the description of my personality was.

How could these results influence my behaviour in a team

Looking through the results of the above tests shows that although sometimes I can create a disturbance in the team
through arguments or competitiveness, it can simply be because I wish to challenge both myself and the team.
This can come across as a negative thing to others, but
because I want to do better as a team and also learn how other people are
thinking, and how they have come to their conclusions.

How should I take this into account when forming a team

When forming a team I need to inform the others that although I can come across rude sometimes, my intentions are good.
I believe I would do better in a team that is varied with people from all walks of life,
as I would use those experiences to help create a stronger team.
